Our Story

Painting that holds up to Perth weather -
and close inspection.

We've been painting Perth homes and businesses for over a decade - long enough to know that a great paint job is mostly about what happens before the first coat goes on.

From Fremantle weatherboards to commercial fitouts in the city, we handle residential, commercial and industrial work right across WA. Every job gets the same prep, the same quality paint, and a crew that turns up when we say we will.

More than 3,000 jobs later, most of our work still comes from people who've had us back - or who were told to call us by someone we've already painted for.

How It Works

How your job runs,
from quote to final coat.

Four steps, no surprises - here's exactly how a job goes from your first call to us packing up and leaving the place spotless.

1

Consultation

We come out to your place (or work from your photos), take a proper look at the job, and talk through what you want and when you need it done.

2

Detailed Quote

A clear, itemised quote - what we'll do and what it costs. No vague "from" prices, no surprise extras turning up on the final invoice.

3

Preparation & Painting

The crew preps every surface properly, then puts the coats on - drop sheets down, edges cut in by hand, your gear kept clean throughout.

4

Final Inspection

We walk the whole job with you before we pack up. If something's not right, we sort it then and there - not "next time we're passing".
